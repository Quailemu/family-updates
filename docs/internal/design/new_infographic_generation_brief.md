# Family Updates Infographic Generation Brief

## Goal

Replace the current message-replacement infographic with a polished, diagrammatic infographic that starts with the real problem:

Communication becomes difficult when communication is used as a filing system.

The image should not rely on people or character illustration. It should show support information becoming mixed inside a generic family messaging group, then being separated into:

1. Family Chat
2. Family Updates
3. Notes & Files

The Notes & Files column should include Shared Notes / Dates people are handling. This is not a Family Office calendar.

## Legal / Brand Constraint

Do not show or name WhatsApp, Meta, or any other third-party messaging brand.

Use neutral wording:

- Family Chat
- family messaging group
- usual messaging apps

No app logos.

## Visual Style

Use the quality level of `assets/infographic-v9.png`:

- polished diagrammatic layout
- simple phone-screen and information-organisation shapes
- strong emoji/icon-led contrast
- soft shadows and clean section boxes
- clean infographic panels
- readable, minimal labels
- calm teal, peach, pale green, muted pink, and navy palette

Avoid:

- people or character scenes
- crude vector styling
- childish cartoon style
- cluttered text
- long paragraphs inside the image
- brand logos
