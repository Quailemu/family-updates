## 1) Contract readiness

- [ ] Final service agreement signed by both parties (not draft/outline only).
- [ ] Scope boundaries match service design (non-urgent social messaging only).
- [ ] Role separation is explicit (Family / Care Hub – Mobile / Care Hub – Office).
- [ ] Suspension and termination clauses are included and approved.
- [ ] Liability, exclusions, and limitation clauses are reviewed by legal counsel.

## 2) Data protection readiness

- [ ] DPA signed (controller = care home, processor = platform).
- [ ] Article 28 processor obligations are explicitly included.
- [ ] Sub-processor list is documented and shared.
- [ ] Sub-processor change notification process and notice period are defined.
- [ ] Breach notification timing is agreed in writing.
- [ ] Data return/deletion obligations are agreed in writing.
- [ ] UK hosting statement and transfer position are documented.

## 3) Insurance readiness (provider)

- [ ] Cyber/privacy liability insurance in force.
- [ ] Professional indemnity / Tech E&O insurance in force.
- [ ] Public liability insurance in force where onsite/device handling risk exists.
- [ ] Current certificates and policy schedules provided to care home.
- [ ] Policy periods cover the full pilot window.

Suggested pilot minimum limits (confirm with broker/legal):
- Cyber/privacy liability: GBP 1,000,000 each claim
- Professional indemnity / Tech E&O: GBP 1,000,000 each claim
- Public liability (if applicable): GBP 1,000,000 each claim

## 4) Incident and governance readiness

- [ ] Named incident contacts for both parties (operational + legal/privacy).
- [ ] Out-of-hours escalation route agreed.
- [ ] Initial acknowledgement window agreed (for example within 24 hours).
- [ ] Ongoing update cadence agreed (for example every 24 hours while active).
- [ ] Post-incident closure summary format agreed.

## 5) Evidence pack

- [ ] Final signed agreement copy stored in Office document pack.
- [ ] Signed DPA copy stored in Office document pack.
- [ ] Insurance certificates stored in Office document pack.
- [ ] Incident response contact sheet stored in Office document pack.
- [ ] Version/date control completed for all above files.
