# Family Terms Summary

This is a short, plain-English summary of the Family Hub terms.

## Key points

- The service is for non-urgent family communication and coordination.
- It is not for urgent, medical, safeguarding, legal, financial, or private matters.
- Family Members use Family Hub unless separately invited into another role.
- Mobile is a distinct practical support role, paid or unpaid.
- Family Office is for the Family Organiser and trusted co-organisers.
- Only the latest item in each channel is kept; there is no message history.
- Access is controlled through the family-side workspace.
- For urgent or sensitive matters, use the agreed direct contact route outside the app.
