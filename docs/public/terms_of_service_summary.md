# Terms & conditions summary (family access)

## Purpose

This is a short summary of the Family app terms. It does not replace the care home’s own policies.

## What the service is

The service provides a simple way to exchange non-urgent, social voice messages between residents and their family or friends. It is not intended for care updates, health information, or safeguarding concerns.

Messages are for non-urgent, social contact only. This service must not be used for care, urgent, or safeguarding matters.

## Role separation

Families use the Family app only. Families do not use Care Hub – Mobile or Care Hub – Office.

## Message replacement

Only the most recent voice message is kept and played. Older messages are automatically replaced. There is no message history.

## Access and management

Access is managed by the care home. The care home decides who can send and receive messages and how the service is used in practice.

## Platform limitations

The service is not intended to provide care or health information. It is not designed to monitor or moderate messages, provide responses, or guarantee privacy in a care-home setting.

## Where to go for help

For care questions, access issues, or disputes, please contact the care home. The service provider supports the care home with the technical platform only.
