# Mobile Q&A

Q: Who should use Care Home Mobile?
A: Carers and staff designated by the care home, using the shared mobile workflow.

Q: How do Mobile staff log in day to day?
A: Mobile is intended for individual staff access using each staff member's own Mobile PIN.

Q: Is email magic-link the normal Mobile login method?
A: No. Email secure link is for first sign-in or expired-session recovery. Routine Mobile access uses each staff member's PIN.

Q: Is Care Home Mobile the same as Office?
A: No. Mobile is the restricted operational view. Office includes broader admin and document tools.

Q: Can Mobile users access contracts and billing?
A: No. Those are Office functions.

Q: Is this for urgent communication?
A: No. The platform is for non-urgent, social voice messages.

Q: Do messages have history?
A: No. Each channel keeps only the latest message.

Q: Can Mobile staff request transcripts?
A: Yes. Transcript assist can be requested when recording. If available, transcript text appears under "Transcript assist" for the current message.

Q: What should staff do before sending a resident message?
A: Follow care-home process and confirm the message has been listened to before sending.

Q: Can staff remove inappropriate contact access?
A: Access changes are managed by the care home using its governance process.

Q: Are safeguarding alerts generated by the platform?
A: No. Safeguarding duties and escalation remain with the care home.

Q: Can we use personal devices for Mobile?
A: Follow care-home policy. Use devices designated by the care home and lock them when unattended.

Q: Why might playback not be immediate?
A: Staff fit playback around care routines and resident needs.

Q: Can Mobile users change resident-contact links?
A: Typically no. These tasks are handled through Office/admin workflows.

Q: What if a family member asks for a care update in a message?
A: Direct them to the care home's normal care communication channels.
