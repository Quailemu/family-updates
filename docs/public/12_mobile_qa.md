# Mobile Q&A

Q: Who should use Mobile?
A: Mobile is for the person providing practical support. This may be a paid carer, professional support worker, spouse, family member, neighbour, or trusted helper.

Q: Is Mobile the same as Family Hub?
A: No. Family Hub is for wider Family Members. Mobile is a distinct practical support role, paid or unpaid.

Q: Is Mobile the same as Family Office?
A: No. Mobile is a reduced-tool view. Family Office includes setup, access, organiser updates, practical requests, and oversight.

Q: How do Mobile users log in day to day?
A: Mobile uses an individual Mobile PIN.

Q: Is email magic-link the normal Mobile login method?
A: No. Email secure link is for first sign-in or expired-session recovery. Routine Mobile access uses the Mobile PIN.

Q: Can Mobile users change family setup or security?
A: No. Those are Family Office functions.

Q: Is this for urgent communication?
A: No. familyupdates.care is for non-urgent family coordination only.

Q: Do messages have history?
A: No. Each channel keeps only the latest item.

Q: Can Mobile users change family access?
A: No. Family access is handled through Family Office.

Q: What if something urgent, medical, safeguarding, legal, or private comes up?
A: Use the agreed direct contact route outside the app.
