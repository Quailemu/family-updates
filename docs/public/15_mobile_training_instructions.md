# Mobile Instructions

Mobile is for the person providing practical support.

This may be a paid carer, professional support worker, spouse, family member, neighbour, or trusted helper. If a family member uses Mobile, they are doing so in a distinct support role, not simply as a Family Member.

Use Mobile for non-urgent family-side coordination and practical support tasks.

Mobile is not Family Office. Mobile users cannot register Family Members, change setup variables, or manage security.

Mobile is not live chat. There are no threads, no archive, no read receipts, and no response-time expectations.

Urgent, medical, safeguarding, legal, financial, private, or time-critical matters must use the agreed direct contact route outside the app.
