# Registering a contact

Use this flow to invite a Family Member/contact into the at-home setup.

## Steps

1. Choose the person in the app.
2. Enter contact details.
3. Confirm local authorisation.
4. Send invite.
5. If needed, retry mapping without sending another invite.

## Notes

- Invite sends access only after local authorisation by the person or Family Organiser.
- Keep relationship labels clear so others understand who is linked.
- Main contact can be set/updated when needed.
