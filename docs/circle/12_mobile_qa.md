# Mobile Q&A

A: No. Mobile supports non-urgent playback and recording only.

Q: Does playback happen instantly?  
A: Not necessarily. Use it around practical routines.

Q: Is this a medical or legal record system?  
A: No. It is a communication tool.

Q: What about urgent or medical concerns?  
A: Use your direct contact routes immediately.
