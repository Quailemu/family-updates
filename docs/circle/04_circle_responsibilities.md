# At-home responsibilities

The person, Family Organiser, and any supporters involved are responsible for local decisions and participation.

- Decide who is invited to take part.
- Decide who can access each person's messages.
- Keep contact details and participation up to date.
- Follow your own safeguarding and escalation arrangements.
- Use normal direct contact routes for urgent or medical matters.

familyupdates.care provides communication infrastructure only and is not monitored in real time.
