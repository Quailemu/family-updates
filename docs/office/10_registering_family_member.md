# Registering a Family Member

## Purpose

Care Hub - Office can invite and register a Family Member for the selected person/resident.

In Stages 1-3, this is an at-home coordination action. The coordinator / family decides who should be added and keeps any registration record outside the app if needed.

In Stage 4, this is a Care Home Office action. The care home remains responsible for authorisation and record-keeping.

## Steps

1. Open Care Hub - Office.
2. Go to "Register a Family Member".
3. Enter first name, last name, and email.
4. Select the person/resident.
5. Confirm the appropriate access decision:
   - Stages 1-3: the coordinator / family has decided the person may be added.
   - Stage 4: the care home has decided the person may be added.
6. Click "Send invitation".

## What Happens Next

The Family Member receives a secure email login link.
The on-screen confirmation records:
- Setup or care home
- Registered by (office staff account)
- Date
- Family email
The Family Member uses the email link to sign in to Family access for the selected person/resident.

## Important Notes

This service is for non-urgent social communication only.
Only appropriate Family Members should be registered.
voicemailcare.com does not decide who is authorised.
In Stages 1-3, the coordinator / family is responsible for access decisions.
In Stage 4, the care home is responsible for identity/authority decisions and for keeping local registration records.
Duplicate registrations are prevented automatically.
