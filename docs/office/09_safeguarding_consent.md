# Safeguarding and consent

## Purpose

This document explains how consent, authority, and safeguarding relate to voicemailcare.com. It is intended for care managers, inspectors, and legal review, and aims to be clear and practical.

## Consent and authority

The care home is responsible for determining resident capacity, obtaining and recording consent, and verifying authority.

The platform does not assess capacity, verify consent, or verify legal authority. It provides the technical tool only.

## Safeguarding

The service is not a safeguarding or alerting system. It does not monitor or flag content, and safeguarding responsibilities remain with the care home.

If the care home chooses to review messages as part of its own processes, that remains a care home decision and responsibility.

## Appropriate use

Messages are social and non-urgent. The service is not intended for care or health communication, and families should contact the care home directly for care-related matters.
